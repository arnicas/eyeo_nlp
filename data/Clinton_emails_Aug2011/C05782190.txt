From: OpsNewsTicker Sent: Tuesday, August 23, 2011 12:16 PM To: NEWS-Mahogany Cc: SES-0 Subject: Libya rebels enter Gaddafi house - Jazeera (Reuters)
TUNIS (Reuters) - Libyan rebels have entered Muammar Gaddafi's house after breaking into his vast Bab alAziziya compound in Tripoli on Tuesday, al-Jazeera television reported, citing sources.
Reuters reporters at the scene said rebels were firing shots into the air from within the compound in celebration.
